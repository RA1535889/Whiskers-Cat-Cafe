Café Rules

These rules help keep our cats and visitors safe.

  • Please do not pick up the cats.
  • No flash photography.
  • Do not wake sleeping cats.
  • Use gentle voices inside the café.
  • Wash or sanitise hands before entering the cat area.
  • Children must be supervised at all times.
